A Web3.O Collective

3.O Labs is an open-door collective of Web3 contributors committed to building sustainable, accessible blockchain-enabled technologies and services.

Looking for “accountability buddies” to explore Web3 together, a group of friends arranged a weekly online discussion about the new prospects blockchain had to offer. As contributors to the decentralized ecosystem of Web3, we do not believe in perfect competition. We welcomed partnerships and collaborations and worked with contributors with various schedule constraints. Within months, the group expanded exponentially as members branched out to onboard newcomers and collaborate on projects.

Emerging from this organic community, 3.O Labs represents a diversity of creatives and technologists who envision a Web3 enabled future across countless industries. Our products and services aim to capture these values and create more meaningful experiences for newcomers, veterans or part-time explorers of Web3.
